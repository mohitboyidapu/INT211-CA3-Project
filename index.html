Mystery Mania

How to Play

  • This is a game of Mystery Boxes
  • Each box contains either a Goal or Mine or Nothing
  • You have to fing all the 3 Goals inside the Boxes
  • Progress is lost when we open a mine
  • Score is given on basis of how many less chances we take to find all 3 goals
